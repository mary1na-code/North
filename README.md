# North

**North** is an AI execution companion that helps you stop overthinking and start doing.

Instead of overwhelming you with endless to-do lists, North guides you through one task at a time based on your goals, priorities, schedule, and progress. Its purpose is simple: eliminate decision fatigue and make consistent execution easier.

## The Problem

Knowing what to do isn't the problem.

The real challenge is the gap between deciding to work and actually starting. During that gap, distractions, procrastination, and analysis paralysis take over.

North is designed to close that gap.

## Vision

Help people spend less time deciding and more time executing.

## Core Principles

- One next action, not twenty.
- Progress over perfection.
- Reduce decision fatigue.
- Build consistency through small wins.
- Use AI to guide execution, not replace thinking.

## MVP Features
