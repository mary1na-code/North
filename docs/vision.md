# Vision
## Why does North exist?
North exists to always bring me back to my goals.
It is an AI execution companion that tells me exactly what to do next based on my goals, schedule, energy, and progress—so I spend less time deciding and more time executing.

## What problem does North aim to solve?
North aims to solve my problem of procrastination which usually goes like this:

``` 03:45
Wake up.

04:00

"I should work on Calma."

04:03

"I'll watch one YouTube video."

8 hours disappear.

Feel guilty.

Repeat tomorrow. ```


## Persona
* Maryann * is a software enginner and freshman computer science student who has a challenge with completing her daily tasks and staying focused on her goals.

# Success Metric / KPI
"What is the smallest version of North that would make tomorrow better than today?"

The smallest version of north that will make tomorrow better than tomorrow is the ability of North to tell me what i have to do at a specific time just like a PA would. North should present 2 button for me to choose to start/contine and for me to reject, if i reject, north should remind me of why i'm suppose to do this and reflect to my mission and ambition and how i will feel if i don't do it.
